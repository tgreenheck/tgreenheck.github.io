Headmaster of Hogwarts & Heads of Houses

“You might belong in Gryffindor,
Where dwell the brave at heart,
Their daring, nerve, and chivalry,
Set Gryffindors apart;
You might belong in Hufflepuff,
Where they are just and loyal,
Those patient Hufflepuffs are true,
And unafraid of toil;
Or yet in wise old Ravenclaw,
If you've a ready mind,
Where those of wit and learning,
Will always find their kind;
Or perhaps in Slytherin,
You'll make your real friends,
These cunning folks use any means
To achieve their ends.”

Minerva McGonagall - Deputy Headmistress + Gryffindor House

After her education, Minverva McGonagall worked for two years at the Ministry of Magic before becoming both the Head Professor of Transfiguration and Head of Gryffindor House. As a teacher, Minerva is a strict disciplinarian, maintaining control of her students with a stern hand that made her someone not to be crossed. After leading the resistance against Lord Voldemort in the Battle of Hogwarts McGonagall becamse Deputy Headmistress of Hogwarts.

Horace Slughorn - Slytherin

Horace Slughorn was friend and colleague of, esteemed former Headmaster, Albus Dumbledore. He is a pure-blood wizard, the Potions Master, and the Head of Slytherin House at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Previous to Severus Snape's succession, he was Potions Professor for over fifty years and Head of Slytherin before retiring in 1981.

Pomona Sprout - Hufflepuff

Pomona Sprout attended the Hogwarts School of Witch Craft and Wizardy in her youth. Excelling at Herbology, a shortwhile after her graudation, she returned to Hogwarts to teach. Sprout is a born edacutor, and is currently serving as Head of Hufflepuff House and Head Professor of Herbology at Hogwarts.

Flilius Flitwick - Ravenclaw

As a part-goblin, part-wizard who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in his youth, Filius Flitwick was sorted into Ravenclaw House, being an intelligent young man and a model student. Sometime after his graduation, Filius returned to Hogwarts and is now the Charms Master, as well as Head of Ravenclaw House.
