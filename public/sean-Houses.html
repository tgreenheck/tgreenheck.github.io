Gryffindor

Are you brave? Do you have valor and a sense of honor? Then Gryffindor is the house for you. Our mascot is the lion and our leader is Profesor McGonagall.

Slytherin

Are you cunning? Ambitions? Then Slytherin is the house for you. Our mascot is the snake, and our leader is Professor Snape.

Hufflepuff

Do you work hard? Are you loyal? Then Hufflepuff is the house for you. Our mascot is the badger, and our leader is Professor Sprout.

Ravenclaw

Are you intelligent? Wise? Then Ravenclaw is the house for you. Our mascot is the eagle, and our leader is Professor Flitwick.

Sorting Process

image taken from: amcnetworks.com

At the beginning of the year, each first-year student wears the sorting hat. This magical hat looks into the heart and mind of the student and selects a house for him or her to be a part of.

House Cup

Throughout the year, houses earn points for academic achievment and lose points for poor behavior. The house with the most points at the end of the year earns the House Cup.
