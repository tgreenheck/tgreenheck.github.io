What's Your House?

Gryffindor

Are you brave? Do you have valor and a sense of honor? Then Gryffindor is the house for you. Our mascot is the lion and our leader is Profesor McGonagall. Notable members of this house are Harry Potter, Sirious Black and former Headmaster Albus Dumbledore.

Slytherin

Are you cunning? Ambitious? Then Slytherin is the house for you. Our mascot is the snake, and our leader is Professor Snape. Notable members of this house include Headmaster Severus Snape, Bellatrix Lestrange and Tom Marvolo Riddle.

Hufflepuff

Do you work hard? Are you loyal? Then Hufflepuff is the house for you. Our mascot is the badger, and our leader is Professor Sprout. Notable members of this house include Cedric Diggory.

Ravenclaw

Are you intelligent? Wise? Then Ravenclaw is the house for you. Our mascot is the eagle, and our leader is Professor Flitwick. Notable members of this house include Luna Lovegood and Filious Flitwick.

Sorting Process

At the beginning of the year, each first-year student wears the sorting hat. This magical hat looks into the heart and mind of the student and selects a house for him or her to be a part of.

House Cup

The House Cup is a yearly award at Hogwarts. It is given at the end of the school year to the House with the most House points. Points are given to Houses by teachers and prefects throughout the year for various things including answering questions correctly and doing good deeds. The winners of school Quidditch games also receive points. Points can be taken away for things such as misbehaving or rule-breaking.
